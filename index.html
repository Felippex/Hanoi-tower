Hanoi Tower

Below you can find the Hanoi Tower.

Your task is to move all rings to the third stick. But there are some rules:

  1. You can take only one ring in one movement.
  2. You can take ring only from the top of the pile.
  3. You cannot put ring with bigger diameter on ring with smaller diameter.
